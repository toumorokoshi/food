Pesto¶

Ingredients¶

  • Salted Cashews: 400g
  • Olive Oil: 120ml
  • Basil: 50g (100g package with stems ripped off)
  • Garlic: 4 Cloves
  • Optional: (Grated Parmesan Cheese)

Instructions¶

Mix all the ingredients together in a blender. If all the ingredients do not fit, mix some in, blend to reduce volume, and continue adding in ingredients. Start with the basil and garlic.
